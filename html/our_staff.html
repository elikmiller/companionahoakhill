Dr. Margaret Leary

Dr. Leary, the clinic owner, completed her undergraduate studies at The University of Texas at Austin. She went on to pursue her Veterinary degree at Texas A&M University, and returned to Austin develop a client base that has lasted for over 20 years! She employs a holistic approach to medicine and enjoys doing laser therapy treatments on her equine patients in her free time. She also enjoys horseback riding and spending quality time at home with her 5 dogs, cats and brood of chickens.

Dr. Mallory Carpenter
